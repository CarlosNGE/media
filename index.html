WOODKID

Discography

"I Love You" is the third single by Woodkid, taken from his debut album The Golden Age. It was co-written by Lemoine himself and by Ambroise Willaume. Arrangements were added by Willaume.

The single was released on 9 February 2013 as a warm-up to the imminent release of the album The Golden Age on 18 March 2013, whereas earlier two singles had been released much earlier, with "Iron" being released on March 28, 2011 and "Run Boy Run" on May 21, 2012.

Yoann Lemoine

Known professionally as Woodkid, is a French video director, graphic designer and singer-songwriter

His most notable work includes his music video direction for Kati Perry's "Teenage Dream" , Tylor Swift's "Born to Die" , and Harry Styles's "Sign of Times".
